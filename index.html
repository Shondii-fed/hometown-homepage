Welcome to Hampton, VA!

"Where history meets the harbor, Hampton, VA offers a vibrant blend of coastal charm, rich heritage, and year-round adventure."

Three of the best attractions in Hampton, VA

Virginia Air and Space Center

Explore the wonders of flight and space at this interactive museum, featuring exhibits on aviation history, space exploration, and hands-on activities for all ages.

Hampton Coliseum

A premier entertainment venue, the Hampton Coliseum hosts concerts, sporting events, and shows throughout the year, making it a hub of excitement in the city.

Oozlefinch Brewery at Fort Monroe

Located in a historic fort, this brewery offers a unique atmosphere with craft beers brewed on-site. Enjoy tastings and tours while soaking in the rich history of the area.
